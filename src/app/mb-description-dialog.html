What are Binaural Beats?

Binaural Beats are an auditory illusion caused by playing two tones of slightly different frequency. When listened to with headphones, the mind interprets it as a single rhythmic tone at a frequency identical to the difference in the two tone's frequencies. This illusion is thought to change the predominant neural frequency of the mind (delta, theta, beta, gamma) in a way that might impact one's state of mind. Popular for those looking to enhance focus, relaxation, or at least harness the placebo effect to their advantage.
